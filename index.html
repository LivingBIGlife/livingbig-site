What is Living BIG

Big animals aren't just charismatic. They're essential.

Protecting them is some of the highest-leverage conservation work on Earth.

Whales change climate.

Their nutrient cycling drives ocean productivity and carbon capture at planetary scale.

Wolves change rivers.

Apex predators shape entire ecosystems through the cascading effects of their presence.

Elephants engineer forests.

Every seed they spread, every path they clear, builds the patterns of their ecosystem.

Each Living BIG expedition builds on a movement — designed to support the people and projects championing these animals, and to keep the audience connected long after the expedition and feature video.
